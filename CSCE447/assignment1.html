For the second question, I would like to share the project I find unhelpful in some way.

5 Health Care Companies will Collect $787 Billion in 2019
    As the picture shows, the author used a bar chart instead of a pie chart to show the percentage of the total revenue of the companies.
    Pie charts make sense to show a parts-to-whole relationship for categorical or nominal data. The slices in the pie typically represent percentages of the total. In this case, the author is trying to show the percentage of the total revenue of the companies, which is a parts-to-whole relationship. Therefore, a pie chart is more suitable for this case.
